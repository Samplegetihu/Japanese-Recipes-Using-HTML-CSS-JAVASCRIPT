Sushi Recipe

How to Make Sushi at Home

Ingredients:

  • Sushi rice: 1 cup sushi rice, 1 1/4 cups water, 2 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp salt
  • Fillings: Sushi-grade fish (tuna, salmon), vegetables (cucumber, avocado), nori (seaweed)
  • Condiments: Soy sauce, wasabi, pickled ginger

Tools:

  • Bamboo sushi mat
  • Sharp knife
  • Rice cooker or pot

Steps:

  1. Cook the Rice: Rinse and cook sushi rice. Season with a vinegar-sugar-salt mix and cool to room temp.
  2. Prepare Fillings: Slice fish and vegetables into thin strips.
  3. Roll Sushi:
    • Place nori on a bamboo mat.
    • Spread rice thinly on nori, leaving an inch at the top.
    • Add fillings, then roll tightly.
    • Wet the top edge to seal.
  4. Slice & Serve: Slice rolls into bite-sized pieces. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Tip

For nigiri, form rice into small ovals and top with fish.

Enjoy your homemade sushi!

Ramen Recipe

How to Make Ramen at Home

Ingredients:

  • Noodles: 200g fresh ramen noodles
  • Broth: 4 cups chicken or pork broth, soy sauce, miso, and garlic
  • Toppings: Soft-boiled egg, sliced pork, green onions, nori, bamboo shoots

Tools:

  • Large pot
  • Soup ladle

Steps:

  1. Prepare Broth: Simmer broth with soy sauce, miso, and garlic for 20 mins.
  2. Cook Noodles: Boil noodles in water until al dente, then drain.
  3. Assemble:
    • Place noodles in a bowl.
    • Ladle hot broth over noodles.
    • Add toppings.
  4. Serve: Garnish and enjoy hot!

Tip

Use homemade broth for an authentic taste.

Enjoy your homemade ramen!

Tempura Recipe

How to Make Tempura at Home

Ingredients:

  • Batter: 1 cup flour, 1 cup cold water, 1 egg
  • Fillings: Shrimp, vegetables (sweet potato, zucchini, mushroom)
  • Condiments: Tempura dipping sauce

Tools:

  • Mixing bowl
  • Deep fryer or pot with oil

Steps:

  1. Prepare Batter: Mix cold water and egg; add flour lightly to avoid lumps.
  2. Heat Oil: Heat oil to 180°C (350°F).
  3. Dip & Fry:
    • Dip shrimp and veggies in batter.
    • Fry until golden and crispy.
  4. Serve: Serve with tempura dipping sauce.

Tip

Keep the batter cold for extra crispiness.

Enjoy your crispy tempura!

Miso Soup Recipe

How to Make Miso Soup at Home

Ingredients:

  • Broth: 4 cups dashi broth
  • Miso Paste: 3 tbsp white or red miso paste
  • Add-ins: Tofu cubes, seaweed, green onions

Tools:

  • Saucepan
  • Soup ladle

Steps:

  1. Heat Dashi: Warm the dashi broth in a saucepan.
  2. Add Miso Paste: Dissolve miso paste in a small amount of broth, then add back to the pan.
  3. Add Tofu & Seaweed: Gently add tofu and seaweed; heat without boiling.
  4. Serve: Garnish with green onions and serve warm.

Tip

Avoid boiling the soup after adding miso paste to preserve its flavor.

Enjoy your miso soup!

Gyoza Recipe

How to Make Gyoza at Home

Ingredients:

  • Filling: 1 cup ground pork, 1/2 cup cabbage (minced), green onion, garlic, ginger, soy sauce
  • Wrappers: Gyoza wrappers
  • Condiments: Soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil for dipping sauce

Tools:

  • Mixing bowl
  • Non-stick skillet

Steps:

  1. Prepare Filling: Mix pork, cabbage, green onion, garlic, ginger, and soy sauce.
  2. Fill Wrappers: Place filling in gyoza wrappers and seal edges with water.
  3. Cook Gyoza:
    • Heat skillet with oil, place gyoza flat-side down.
    • Add water, cover and steam for a few minutes until water evaporates.
  4. Serve: Serve with dipping sauce.

Tip

Use a pleated fold to seal gyoza for a traditional look.

Enjoy your homemade gyoza!

Takoyaki Recipe

How to Make Takoyaki at Home

Ingredients:

  • Batter: 1 cup flour, 1 1/2 cups dashi, 2 eggs
  • Fillings: Octopus pieces, green onion, pickled ginger
  • Condiments: Takoyaki sauce, mayonnaise, bonito flakes

Tools:

  • Takoyaki pan
  • Skewers or chopsticks

Steps:

  1. Prepare Batter: Mix flour, dashi, and eggs to form a smooth batter.
  2. Heat Pan: Oil takoyaki pan and heat.
  3. Cook Takoyaki:
    • Pour batter into pan, add octopus and fillings.
    • Turn balls with skewers until golden.
  4. Serve: Drizzle with sauce, mayo, and top with bonito flakes.

Tip

Use a generous amount of oil for a crispy texture.

Enjoy your takoyaki!

Katsudon Recipe

How to Make Katsudon at Home

Ingredients:

  • Pork Cutlet: 1 pork cutlet, flour, egg, panko breadcrumbs
  • Sauce: 1/2 cup dashi, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp mirin
  • Other: 1/2 onion, 1 egg, steamed rice

Tools:

  • Frying pan
  • Small pot

Steps:

  1. Prepare Pork Cutlet: Coat cutlet in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs, then fry until golden.
  2. Simmer Sauce: Combine dashi, soy sauce, and mirin in a pan with sliced onion; simmer until soft.
  3. Add Cutlet & Egg:
    • Place fried cutlet in sauce.
    • Pour beaten egg over and cook until just set.
  4. Serve: Serve over rice and enjoy.

Tip

Use fresh panko for a crispier cutlet.

Enjoy your katsudon!

Onigiri Recipe

How to Make Onigiri at Home

Ingredients:

  • Rice: 2 cups sushi rice
  • Fillings: Pickled plum, salmon, tuna mayo
  • Wrapper: Nori sheets

Tools:

  • Mixing bowl
  • Plastic wrap or mold

Steps:

  1. Cook Rice: Cook and cool sushi rice.
  2. Shape Onigiri: Place filling in the center of rice ball; shape with hands or mold.
  3. Wrap with Nori: Wrap rice ball with a piece of nori.
  4. Serve: Serve as is or with dipping sauce.

Tip

Use wet hands to prevent rice from sticking.

Enjoy your onigiri!

Yaki Dango Recipe

Grilled skewered rice dumplings with sweet sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup glutinous rice flour
  • 3/4 cup water
  • Soy sauce and sugar for sauce

Steps:

  1. Mix rice flour and water to form a smooth dough.
  2. Shape into small balls and steam for 10 minutes.
  3. Skewer dumplings and grill until lightly charred.
  4. Brush with a sweet soy sauce glaze before serving.

Tamagoyaki Recipe

Japanese rolled omelette, soft and sweet.

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp mirin

Steps:

  1. Beat eggs with sugar, soy sauce, and mirin.
  2. Pour a thin layer into a pan and cook until slightly set.
  3. Roll up and push to the side, then add more egg and repeat.
  4. Once rolled, slice and serve.

Zenzai Recipe

Sweet red bean soup served with mochi.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup azuki beans
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 small mochi (rice cakes)

Steps:

  1. Cook azuki beans in water until soft, then drain and add fresh water.
  2. Stir in sugar and simmer until beans are tender and mixture thickens.
  3. Grill or toast mochi until puffed, then add to soup and serve warm.

Oshiruko Recipe

Sweet azuki bean soup, often served with rice cakes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup azuki beans
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 mochi (rice cakes)

Steps:

  1. Boil azuki beans until soft, then add sugar and continue to simmer.
  2. In a separate pan, heat mochi until soft or slightly toasted.
  3. Serve bean soup hot with mochi on top or on the side.

Maki Recipe

Classic rolled sushi with a variety of fillings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sushi rice, cooked
  • 2 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1/4 cucumber, sliced thinly
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 100g fresh fish (tuna or salmon), sliced
  • Soy sauce and wasabi (for serving)

Steps:

  1. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat, shiny side down.
  2. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice evenly over the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
  3. Arrange cucumber, avocado, and fish slices along the center of the rice.
  4. Roll up tightly using the mat, pressing firmly to seal.
  5. Slice into bite-sized pieces and serve with soy sauce and wasabi.

Chawanmushi Recipe

Steamed egg custard with savory toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup dashi (Japanese broth)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp mirin
  • Small pieces of chicken, shrimp, or mushrooms (for toppings)

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, gently whisk eggs with dashi, soy sauce, and mirin.
  2. Strain the mixture to remove bubbles, ensuring a smooth custard.
  3. Place chicken, shrimp, or mushrooms in small heatproof cups.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the toppings and cover each cup with foil.
  5. Steam for 10–15 minutes until the custard is set. Serve warm.

Tonkatsu Recipe

Crispy breaded pork cutlet served with shredded cabbage.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pork cutlets
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying
  • Shredded cabbage (for serving)

Steps:

  1. Season pork cutlets with salt and pepper, then coat with flour.
  2. Dip cutlets in beaten egg, then coat with panko breadcrumbs.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry the cutlets until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Drain on paper towels, slice, and serve with shredded cabbage.
